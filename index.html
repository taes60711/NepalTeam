About Boudha Stupa

Boudha, or Boudhanath, in Kathmandu, is one of the largest spherical stupas in the world. It is a sacred Buddhist site representing enlightenment and houses relics of Buddha. The area is home to many monasteries and Tibetan communities.

About Kathmandu Durbar Square

Kathmandu Durbar Square, also known as Basantapur or Hanuman Dhoka, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. It showcases Nepal's ancient architecture, temples, and royal palaces, symbolizing its cultural history. Despite the 2015 earthquake, it remains a vibrant and active historical site.
